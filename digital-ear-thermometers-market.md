<p><p>The digital ear thermometers market is categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometers utilize infrared technology to measure ear temperature without directly touching the skin, providing quick and hygienic readings. In contrast, contact-type thermometers require insertion into the ear canal to obtain accurate temperature readings. Both types cater to different user preferences, with non-contact thermometers often favored for convenience and speed, while contact types are known for their precision and reliability in clinical settings.</p></p>
