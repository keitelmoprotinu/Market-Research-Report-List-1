<p><p>The secured encryption chip market is primarily categorized into contact and contactless segments. Contact chips require physical contact between the chip and a reader for data exchange, commonly used in smart cards and payment terminals. Conversely, contactless chips utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, allowing data transmission over short distances without direct contact, ideal for applications like mobile payments and access control. Both types enhance transaction security, safeguarding sensitive information in various sectors, including finance, healthcare, and transportation.</p></p>
