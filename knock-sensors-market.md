<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Acoustic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knock sensors market is primarily divided into piezoelectric and acoustic sensor types. Piezoelectric sensors convert mechanical vibrations into electrical signals, effectively detecting engine knocking through changes in pressure and temperature. On the other hand, acoustic sensors utilize sound wave analysis to identify knock events, offering high sensitivity and response speed. Both types are crucial for optimizing engine performance and reducing emissions, catering to the automotive industry’s need for more efficient and reliable engine management systems.</p></p>
