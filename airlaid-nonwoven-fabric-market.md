<p><p>The airlaid nonwoven fabric market is categorized into three main types: latex-bonded, thermal-bonded, and multi-bonded fabrics. Latex-bonded fabrics utilize liquid latex to bond fibers, offering flexibility and softness, ideal for personal hygiene products. Thermal-bonded fabrics use heat to fuse fibers, providing strength and durability, suitable for applications like wipes and medical textiles. Multi-bonded fabrics combine multiple bonding techniques to enhance performance, making them versatile for various end uses, including automotive and consumer goods. Each type caters to specific market needs.</p></p>
