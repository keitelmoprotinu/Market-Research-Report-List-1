<p><ul><li>Single-walled Nanotubes (SWNTs)</li><li>Multi-walled Nanotubes (MWNTs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon nanotube powder for lithium batteries primarily comes in two types: single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) and multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, offering high electrical conductivity and impressive mechanical strength. In contrast, MWNTs consist of multiple layered tubes, providing enhanced structural stability and larger surface area, which improves energy storage and conductivity. Both types are utilized to enhance the performance and efficiency of lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
