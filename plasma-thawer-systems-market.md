<p><ul><li>Dry Plasma Thawer</li><li>Wet Plasma Thawer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma thawer systems are essential for rapidly and safely thawing frozen plasma for medical use. The market is primarily divided into two types: dry plasma thawers and wet plasma thawers. Dry plasma thawers use heated air or plates to uniformly thaw plasma without direct contact with water, minimizing contamination risks. In contrast, wet plasma thawers use water baths to provide consistent temperatures, ensuring efficient thawing but requiring careful monitoring to avoid temperature fluctuations. Both systems cater to different operational needs in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
