<p><p>Pharmacy Benefit Managers (PBMs) operate within two market types. In a Type I market, PBMs act as intermediaries between payers (like insurance companies) and pharmacies, negotiating drug prices and managing formularies to control costs and improve access for patients. In contrast, a Type II market involves PBMs in a more competitive landscape, where multiple PBMs offer services to various payers, creating opportunities for differentiation based on pricing strategies, technology, and customer service. Both types aim to optimize medication management.</p></p>
