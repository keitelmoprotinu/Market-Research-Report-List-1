<p><ul><li>Thick Film Chip Resistor</li><li>Thin Film Chip Resistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive grade chip resistor market features two primary types: thick film and thin film chip resistors. Thick film chip resistors are known for their robustness and cost-effectiveness, utilizing a resistive paste applied to a ceramic substrate, making them suitable for high-volume applications. In contrast, thin film chip resistors offer greater precision and stability, using a thin resistive layer deposited on the substrate, ideal for applications requiring enhanced performance and reliability in automotive systems. Both types meet stringent automotive standards.</p></p>
