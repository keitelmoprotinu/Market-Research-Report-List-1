<p><p>Catalytic gas detectors are critical in monitoring flammable gases to enhance safety in industrial environments. The market comprises two primary types: point detectors and open path detectors. Point detectors focus on specific locations to sense gas concentrations, providing precise readings and rapid response in confined spaces. In contrast, open path detectors analyze gas presence over a broader area, using beams of light to detect gas concentrations along a path, making them effective for large-scale monitoring and outdoor applications.</p></p>
