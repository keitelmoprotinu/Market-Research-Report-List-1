<p><p>Mescal is categorized into various types based on aging processes, primarily including Mescal Joven, Mescal Reposado, and Mescal Añejo. Mescal Joven is a youthful spirit, typically unaged or lightly rested, showcasing the purest agave flavors. Mescal Reposado is aged for a minimum of two months to one year in wooden barrels, allowing it to develop richer, more complex flavors. Mescal Añejo, aged for over a year, offers a deeper character, blending the essence of agave with the influence of wood.</p></p>
