<p><strong>The VRLA Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) battery market comprises two primary types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries, featuring a fiberglass mat that absorbs electrolyte, provide higher power output, faster recharge times, and better cycling capabilities, making them ideal for high-drain applications. Gel batteries, on the other hand, contain silica-based gel electrolyte, offering superior deep discharge and temperature tolerance, making them suitable for applications requiring longevity and durability in harsh conditions. Both types emphasize safety and maintenance-free operation.</p></p>
