<p><p>The infrared detection equipment market comprises various technologies tailored for specific applications. Mercury cadmium telluride (MCT) is known for its sensitivity in detecting infrared wavelengths, making it ideal for military and space applications. Indium gallium arsenide (InGaAs) offers excellent performance in near-infrared detection, suitable for telecommunications. Pyroelectric sensors detect thermal changes and are widely used in motion detection. Thermopiles convert heat into electrical signals, often used in non-contact temperature measurements, while microbolometers, effective in thermal imaging, serve diverse sectors like security and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
